Portfolio Style Guide

Overall Formatting

The site uses Arial, Helvetica, and sans-serif fonts. The background is light gray, and the text color is dark gray.

Site Header / Branding

The header uses a dark blue background with white text and padding to make the title stand out.

Navigation Links

The navigation bar uses a darker blue background. Links are white, centered, and become underlined when hovered over.

Content Section

The main content area has a white background, centered layout, and padding for spacing. Section titles are blue.

Site Footer

The footer uses a dark blue background with white centered text and padding.
